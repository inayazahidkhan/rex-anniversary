Dear Rex,

I keep trying to find the right way to start this, and I keep landing back on the same truth — two months ago, a friendship I already loved quietly turned into something I now can't imagine my days without.

You didn't come into my life with grand gestures. You came in slowly, patiently, one call and one silly game at a time, until one day I looked up and realized you'd become the person I run to first, with the good news and the bad.

Thank you for every "mai hu na," for staying even when I made it hard, for turning ordinary days into memories I'll keep long after I've forgotten what we even talked about. Thank you for being patient with my heart while it learned to trust yours.

I don't know what the next months will look like. I just know I want to find out with you — one call, one game, one ordinary beautiful day at a time.

Forever your Inaya.
